Tennis RPO

Smarter Decisions. Stronger Teams.

A Practical Framework for High School Tennis Coaching

Tennis RPO adapts the Read-Pass-Option concept from football to help coaches and players make better decisions on court. It’s not a system to replace your style — it’s a shared language to support it.

Why This Matters for High School Tennis

  • Builds player confidence through clear decision-making
  • Forges team identity with shared vocabulary
  • Makes practices more productive by layering mental focus into drills
  • Improves peer learning with a common understanding

The Core: Read → Decide → Execute

READ

  • Where is your opponent?
  • What are their tendencies?
  • What’s the score?

DECIDE

  • What’s working?
  • What’s the best percentage play?
  • How much risk is right now?

EXECUTE

  • Commit to your choice
  • Focus on quality execution
  • Prepare for the next read

Decision Cards: The Heart of Tennis RPO

These cards simulate real match scenarios and train players to think strategically. They're simple, effective, and classroom-ready.

Each card helps players:

  1. Read the situation (position, score, tendencies)
  2. Evaluate multiple options
  3. Decide and explain the best play

Understanding Leverage Points

These are the moments that swing a match — not just “big points,” but situations that demand smart decisions.

  • Early Game Points – Set the tone early
  • Deuce Points – Especially in no-ad format
  • Momentum Shifts – Reverse match flow
  • Adjustment Moments – Respond to what’s changing
