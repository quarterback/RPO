Tennis RPO

A simple way to teach better decisions on court

Make Smart Decisions. Build Confident Players.

Tennis RPO borrows the "Read-Pass-Option" idea from football and applies it to high school tennis. It's not a system to replace your coaching—just a shared language to make your team smarter and more confident on court.

Browse Resources

Why It Works for High School Tennis

  • Builds confidence in player decision-making
  • Creates team identity with shared language
  • Makes practices more effective by integrating mental focus
  • Encourages peer support through common understanding

The RPO Model: Read → Decide → Execute

READ

  • Where is your opponent?
  • What are their tendencies?
  • What’s the score situation?

DECIDE

  • What shot gives you the best chance?
  • How much risk makes sense right now?
  • What’s been working?

EXECUTE

  • Commit fully to the shot
  • Focus on quality execution
  • Get ready to read again

Practice Pattern: The 2–1

This three-shot pattern gives players a simple plan:

  1. Deep ball to opponent’s backhand
  2. Wide angle off the short return
  3. Finish into the open court
